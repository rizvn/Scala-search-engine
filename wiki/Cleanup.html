Cleanup

Many cleanup templates listed below need their very own minor cleanup. They add tagged pages to a category, unfortunately also lists with examples. To disable this replace
...<includeonly>[[Category:etc.]]</includeonly> by
...<includeonly>{{{category|[[Category:etc.]]}}}</includeonly>.

Tag Text that will be shown (and usage notes)
{{cleanup}}

category links talk view
To meet Wikipedia's quality standards, this article or section may require cleanup.
Please discuss this issue on the talk page, and/or replace this tag with a more specific message. Editing help is available.
  • This applies to general problems not addressed by other tags.
  • In the future, please use cleanup-date, below, instead.

Merging and splitting

The following tags should be placed atop the articles or sections to which they refer.

Tag Text that will be shown (and usage notes)
{{split}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article be split into multiple articles accessible from a disambiguation page. (Discuss)
  • Place this at the top of an article for which a split into two or more new articles is proposed.
{{splitunrelated}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article be split into multiple distinct articles. (Discuss)
  • Place this at the top of an article for which a split into two or more new articles is proposed, and where they are distinct enough that a disambiguation page is not appropriate.
{{splitsection}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this section be split into a new article. (Discuss)
  • Place this at the top of one or more sections for which a split into a new article is proposed.
{{splitsection|new title}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this section be split into a new article titled new title. (Discuss)
  • Optionally, you may include a suggested title for the new article as a parameter.
{{splitsections|new title}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that some sections of this article be split into a new article entitled new title. (Discuss)
  • Place this at the top of an article for which a split of multiple sections into a new article is proposed.
  • If possible, instead use {{splitsection}} or {{splitsection|new title}} in each of the sections that should be split into the new article.
{{mergesplit|dest items}}
links talk view

This article should be merged into or split into the following resources: dest items. (Discuss)


  • Use this when an article can dismantled into one or more targets.
  • Article is added to both merge & split categories.
{{merge|other item}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with other item. ([[|Discuss]])
  • Place this at the top of both articles or sections that are to be merged. Use the other item's name as the parameter. A link will be generated automatically.
  • If it is known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tags {{mergeto|dest item}} and {{mergefrom|source item}} (or one of the sectional variants) instead.
{{merge-multiple|s1|s2|s3}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with s1, s2 and s3 (Discuss)
  • If the proposed source is an entire article, place this at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged materials.
  • Place a similar tag {{mergefrom-multiple|s1|s2|s3}} at the top of the source articles or sections.
  • This template is for proposing the merger of from 1 up to 20 articles into a single page. It formats a proper list of links.
  • Ideally, a "discuss" parameter should be specified to direct the discussion to another page.
{{mergeto|dest item|talk page}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article or section be merged into dest item. (Discuss)
  • Place this at the top of the article or section that is to have its content transferred to another page.
  • If the proposed source is an entire article, place the tag {{mergefrom|source article}} at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • If the proposed source is one specific section of article, place the tag {{mergesection|source article|source section}} at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • If proposing that multiple sections of a single article (but not the entire article) be merged into another article, place the tag {{mergesections|source article}} at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • Consider adding new material to the article or section that is the parameter of this tag (i.e. [[dest item]]), instead of the article or section that contains this template message.
  • If it is not known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tag {{merge|other item}} instead.
{{mergefrom|source|talk page}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that source be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)
  • If the proposed source is an entire article, place this at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • Place the tag {{mergeto|dest item}} at the top of the source article or section.
  • If a merger of multiple articles into a single page is being proposed, use {{mergefrom-multiple|source 1|src2|src3}}.
  • By default, the discussion is directed to the talk page of the article this tag is placed on. Optionally, a second parameter can be specified to direct the discussion to another page.
  • If it is not known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tag {{merge|other item}} instead.
{{mergefrom-multiple|s1|s2|s3}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that s1, s2 and s3 be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)
  • If the proposed source is an entire article, place this at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged materials.
  • Place the tag {{mergeto|dest item}} at the top of the source articles or sections.
  • This template is for proposing the merger of from 1 up to 20 articles into a single page. It formats a proper list of links.
  • By default, the discussion is directed to the talk page of the article this tag is placed on. Optionally, a "discuss" parameter can be specified to direct the discussion to another page.
  • If it is not known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tag {{merge|other item}} instead.
{{mergesection|source|section}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that the section section from the article source be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)
  • If the proposed source is one specific section of an article, place this at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • Place the tag {{mergeto|dest item}} at the top of the source article or section.
  • If it is not known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tag {{merge|other item}} instead.
{{mergesections|source}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that multiple sections of source be merged into this article or section. (Discuss)
  • If proposing that multiple sections of a single article (but not the entire article) be merged into another article, place this at the top of the article or section that is to receive the merged material.
  • Place the tag {{mergeto|dest item}} at the top of the each source section.
  • If it is not known which article or section is to receive the merged material, use the tag {{merge|other item}} instead.
{{mergesectionto|dest item}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this section be merged into dest item. ([[|Discuss]])
  • Use this tag at the top of the source section.
  • Place the tag {{mergesection|source|section}} at the top of the destination article or at the top of the destination section.
{{merging|dest item}}

category links talk view
This article is in the process of being merged into dest item, and may be outdated.

If necessary, please edit only the article mentioned above, as this article's content may be turned into a redirect.

Please see the relevant discussion on this article's talk page and/or the destination article's talk page.
  • Place this at the top of a source article for which a merger has begun (but has not yet been completed).
{{mergedisputed|other item}}

category links talk view
It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with other item. ([[|Discuss]])
  • Use this template if there is a dispute as to whether or not two articles should be merged.
  • Place this at the top of both articles or sections that are to be merged. Use the other item's name as the parameter. A link will be generated automatically.

{{Move to Wiktionary}}

category links talk view

This page is a candidate to be copied to Wiktionary using the Transwiki process.
The information in this article appears to be more suited for a dictionary rather than an encyclopedia. Wikipedia is not a dictionary, but Wiktionary is. Please verify that this article meets the Wiktionary criteria for inclusion and check that Wiktionary does not already have an article on this word or phrase. If this article can be modified to be more than a dictionary entry, please do so and remove this message.
{{Move to Wikiquote}}

category links talk view

This page is a candidate to be moved to Wikiquote using the Transwiki process.
If the page can be expanded into an encyclopedic article, rather than a list of quotes, please do so and remove this message.
{{Move to Wikisource}}

category links talk view

This page is a candidate to be copied to Wikisource
If the page can be edited into an encyclopedic article, rather than merely a copy of the source text, please do so and remove this message. Otherwise, you can help by formatting it per the Wikisource guidelines in preparation for being imported to Wikisource by a Wikisource admin. However if this source text is not in English it will have to be copied using the transwiki process.
{{Move to Wikibooks}}

category links talk view

This page is a candidate to be copied to Wikibooks using the Transwiki process.
If the page can be re-written into an encyclopedic article, please do so and remove this message.

Before you move this content to Wikibooks, verify that it conforms to Wikibooks policies of acceptable content at What is Wikibooks?. Often content unacceptable to Wikipedia may not be acceptable on Wikibooks either. Also note that failure to follow the Transwiki guidelines when copying content may result in the content transferred to Wikibooks being simply deleted as vandalism and spam.

If the page can be edited into an encyclopedic article, rather than simply a recipe, please do so and remove this message.

Failure to follow the Transwiki guidelines when copying content may result in the content transferred to Wikibooks being simply deleted as vandalism and spam.

{{Move to gaming wiki}}

category links talk view

This article or section is a candidate to be moved to a gaming wiki.
Articles on computer and video games should not list minutiae or give a detailed description of how to play the game. Such topics should be moved to a gaming wiki, such as StrategyWiki, Encyclopedia Gamia, or GameInfo.

If the article or section can be re-written to give an encyclopedic overview, please do so and remove this message. See What Wikipedia is not and WikiProject Computer and video games for further details on content guidelines.

Tag Text that will be shown (and usage notes)
{{notenglish}}

category links talk view
This article needs translation.
Please see this article's entry on Pages needing translation into English for discussion. If the article is not rewritten in English within the next two weeks it will be listed for deletion and possibly moved to a Wikipedia in its current language.

If you have just labeled this page as needing translation, please add
{{ subst:Needtrans| pg=Template messages/Cleanup| Language=unknown| Comments= }} ~~~~
to the bottom of this section of Pages needing translation into English.

  • This always should be supplemented by a note on Wikipedia:Pages needing translation into English.
  • This should be used only for articles that are completely or almost completely in a foreign language.
  • Note that if the issue isn't dealt with in two weeks, the article goes to Articles for deletion.
{{TranslatePassage|language}}

category links talk view
A passage in this article needs to be translated from language into English.
If you are confident enough in your fluency of English and language, please feel free to add a translation.
  • This always should be supplemented by a note on Wikipedia:Pages needing translation into English.
  • This should be used only for articles that are completely or almost completely in a foreign language.
  • Note that if the issue isn't dealt with in two weeks, the article goes to Articles for deletion.
{{cleanup-translation}}

category links talk view
This article contains translated text and needs attention from someone approaching dual fluency.
Please see this article's entry on Pages needing translation into English for discussion.

If you have just labeled this page as needing such attention, please add
{{subst:Duflu| pg=Template messages/Cleanup| Language= | Comments= }}--~~~~
to the bottom of Pages needing translation into English.

  • This always should be supplemented by a note on Wikipedia:Pages needing translation into English.
  • This should be used for articles that still have translation issues, but have enough English-language content that they are not subject to deletion for that reason.
